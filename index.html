About Us

It's as simple as pie. Judgement free, inclusive food relief.

Forget the hassles of complex registration processes. Just download the app, choose your location and how you want to access food for the day - either as a hot meal or as pantry staples, and pick up from a food locker near you.

You'll find our lockers all over the city and our mobile app is your gateway to access food with anonymity and autonomy.
